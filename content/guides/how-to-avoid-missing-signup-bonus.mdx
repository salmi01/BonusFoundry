The safest way to avoid missing a signup bonus is to confirm the offer before account creation, apply the code or link at the required step, and send only after the provider shows terms that match your transfer.

## Direct answer

Signup bonuses fail most often when the account is created too early, the code or link is not applied, the first transfer does not qualify, verification is incomplete, or the offer is not available for the sender country, destination, or payment method.

## Practical steps

1. Read the offer in the provider app or website.
2. Confirm that your sender country and destination country are eligible.
3. Check the minimum transfer amount and deadline.
4. Use the referral link or code before signup.
5. Take screenshots of the offer terms if the bonus is important to you.
6. Confirm the bonus is still visible before sending the first transfer.
7. Keep the transfer receipt until the provider resolves the reward.

## Checklist before signup

- Offer is active for your country and account type.
- Referral link, referral code, or promo code is applied at the right step.
- Destination country and payout method match the terms.
- Minimum transfer and expiry date are clear.
- Identity verification requirements are understood.
- The total cost still makes sense after fees and exchange rate.

## Common mistakes

- Creating an account before opening the referral link.
- Using an expired code from an old page.
- Sending to a destination excluded by the campaign.
- Ignoring verification or account review delays.
- Assuming a bonus applies to existing users.

## FAQ

### What should I save before sending?

Save the offer screen, referral link or code, signup date, and transfer receipt.

### What if the bonus does not arrive?

Check the terms first, then contact the provider with screenshots and transfer details.

### Can I add a signup bonus after creating the account?

Usually no. Many providers require the link or code before account creation or before the first transfer.

### Can verification delay a signup bonus?

Yes. Identity checks, payment authentication, address review, or transfer review can delay both the transfer and the reward.
