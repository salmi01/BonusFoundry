A welcome bonus usually needs to be claimed before or during signup, or before the first qualifying transfer. Check the provider's live offer, apply the required link or code at the right step, and confirm the transfer qualifies before sending money.

## Direct answer

To claim a welcome bonus, start from the provider's current offer, use the referral link, referral code, promo code, or app offer before the qualifying action, complete verification, and send a transfer that meets the provider's terms.

## Practical steps

1. Choose the provider and route you want to use.
2. Open the referral link or copy the code before creating the account.
3. Check the live offer amount, expiry, minimum transfer, and eligible countries.
4. Complete identity verification if required.
5. Send the qualifying first transfer and keep the receipt.
6. Wait for the provider's stated reward timing before treating the bonus as missing.

## Checklist before claiming

- Offer applies to your sender country and destination.
- Link or code is applied before signup or first transfer.
- Transfer amount and payout method match the terms.
- Identity or payment verification can be completed.
- Reward type is clear: fee discount, transfer credit, gift card, cashback, or other benefit.
- Reward timing and expiry are clear.

## Common mistakes

- Sending less than the minimum transfer amount.
- Using a destination country excluded by the offer.
- Assuming a referral reward is paid instantly.
- Comparing only the bonus and ignoring fee and exchange rate.
- Creating the account before applying the offer.

## FAQ

### Can I claim a welcome bonus after signup?

Usually no. Some providers may review account-specific cases, but many require the code or link before account creation.

### Is a welcome bonus automatic?

Not always. Some bonuses appear during checkout, while others are applied after a qualifying transfer or provider review.

### Can existing users claim a welcome bonus?

Usually welcome bonuses are for new users. Existing users should use only offers shown for their account.

### What should I do if the welcome bonus is missing?

Check the code or link, offer terms, transfer status, verification status, and reward timing. Contact the provider with screenshots and the transfer receipt if the terms appear to be met.
