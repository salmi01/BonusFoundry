A welcome bonus usually needs to be claimed before or during signup. If you create the account first and look for a code later, the provider may not let you add it.

## Practical steps

1. Choose the provider and route you want to use.
2. Open the referral link or copy the code before creating the account.
3. Check the live offer amount, expiry, minimum transfer, and eligible countries.
4. Complete identity verification if required.
5. Send the qualifying first transfer and keep the receipt.

## Mistakes to avoid

- Sending less than the minimum transfer amount.
- Using a destination country excluded by the offer.
- Assuming a referral reward is paid instantly.
- Comparing only the bonus and ignoring fee and exchange rate.

- [Taptap Send](/providers/taptap-send) users should check sender and destination country eligibility.

## FAQ

### Can I claim a welcome bonus after signup?

Usually no. Some providers may review account-specific cases, but many require the code or link before account creation.

### Is a welcome bonus guaranteed?

No. Eligibility depends on the provider's current rules, verification, country, transfer amount, and payment method.
