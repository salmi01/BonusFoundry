Before using a money transfer referral link, confirm that it opens the provider's signup flow, that the offer applies to your account and route, and that you understand the qualifying transfer requirements before sharing personal information.

## Direct answer

A referral link can be useful when it takes you to the provider's real signup or app flow and the offer matches your country, destination, transfer amount, payment method, and account status.

## Practical steps

1. Check the provider name and official domain or app store listing.
2. Read the current reward terms before entering personal information.
3. Confirm sender country, recipient country, payment method, and minimum amount.
4. Avoid anyone asking for passwords, card details, or identity documents outside the provider app.
5. Keep a copy of the offer terms before sending your first transfer.

## Checklist before opening or using a link

- The provider name and domain match the real provider.
- The link opens the provider website, official app, or app store.
- The offer applies to the sender country and destination.
- New-user, existing-user, and first-transfer rules are clear.
- Minimum transfer, expiry, reward timing, and payout method are clear.
- You are not asked to send documents or passwords outside the provider's secure flow.

## Common mistakes

- Trusting fixed reward claims without checking the provider's current offer screen.
- Using a link from a page pretending to be official provider support.
- Sharing sensitive documents outside the provider's secure flow.
- Assuming a referral link applies to existing accounts.
- Creating an account before opening the link.

## FAQ

### Are referral links safe?

They can be safe when they lead to the provider's official signup flow. Avoid third parties that ask for private account details.

### Can a referral link stop working?

Yes. Offers can expire or become unavailable by country, campaign, or provider rule.

### Can I use a referral link after signup?

Usually no. Many providers require the link before account creation or before the first qualifying transfer.

### What should I do if the link opens the wrong page?

Close it, do not enter personal details, and open the provider directly. Use only links that reach the provider's real site, app, or app store listing.
