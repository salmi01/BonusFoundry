## Why Didn't My TapTap Send Bonus Credit Apply?

Bonus credit may not apply if the offer was not active for the account, the transfer did not match the promotion criteria, or the code was not entered at the right time.

Referral credit may not apply if the referred friend was already a TapTap Send user, did not use the code on their first transfer, did not complete a successful eligible transfer, or redeemed the code after the allowed period.

Payment verification can also stop a transfer from completing. TapTap Send says card transfers may require 3D Secure authentication through the sender's bank.
