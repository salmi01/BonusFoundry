Welcome bonuses are conditional. A missing code, wrong signup path, excluded route, incomplete verification, payment issue, or transfer that does not meet the terms can stop the reward from appearing.

## Direct answer

The most common reasons are that the code was not applied, the account was not new, identity checks were incomplete, the transfer was below the minimum amount, or the offer was not available in that country or corridor.

## Checks to make

- Confirm the referral link or code was used before account creation.
- Check whether your first transfer met the minimum amount.
- Make sure verification is complete.
- Review the offer expiry date.
- Look for provider messages about excluded payment methods or countries.

## Practical steps

1. Compare your transfer receipt with the offer terms.
2. Check whether the transfer is completed, pending, cancelled, or under review.
3. Confirm that the code or link was accepted before signup.
4. Wait for the provider's stated reward timing.
5. Contact support with exact dates, screenshots, and transfer details.

## Checklist before contacting support

- Signup date and transfer date are available.
- Offer screen, code, or referral link is saved.
- Transfer receipt shows amount, destination, and delivery method.
- Verification status is complete or documented.
- Provider reward timing has passed.
- Support message includes the exact issue and screenshots.

## Common mistakes

- Contacting support without the offer screenshot or transfer receipt.
- Assuming an advertised offer applied to your country.
- Forgetting that identity verification can delay rewards.
- Sending a different amount or payout method from the offer terms.

## Provider examples

- [Taptap Send](/providers/taptap-send) users should check corridor eligibility.
- [Wise](/providers/wise) users should check the invitation and qualifying action.
- [Western Union](/providers/western-union) users should confirm whether the offer applied online, in-app, or at an agent location.
- [Ria](/providers/ria) users should check whether the code or promo appeared in the live flow before payment.

## When to contact support

Contact the provider if you believe you met every requirement. Include the signup date, transfer date, referral code or link used, and any screenshots of the offer terms.

## Keep expectations realistic

A referral guide can explain common rules, but only the provider can confirm account-specific eligibility.

## FAQ

### Can Bonus Foundry fix a missing bonus?

No. Bonus Foundry is independent and cannot access provider accounts or approve rewards.

### Can a bonus be rejected even after a transfer?

Yes. Providers may reject a reward if the account, route, amount, or timing does not match the terms.

### Should I send another transfer to trigger the bonus?

Only if the provider's terms say another qualifying transfer is required. Do not send extra money just to chase a bonus without clear provider instructions.

### Can verification delay a reward?

Yes. Identity, address, payment, source-of-funds, or transfer review can delay reward timing.
