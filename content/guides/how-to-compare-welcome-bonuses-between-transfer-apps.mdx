The largest advertised welcome bonus is not always the best deal. Compare the total transfer result, eligibility requirements, reward timing, fees, exchange rate, and delivery method before choosing an app.

## Direct answer

Compare welcome bonuses by total outcome, not headline reward. A smaller bonus can be better if the exchange rate, fee, payout method, transfer speed, and eligibility rules are stronger for your route.

## Practical steps

1. Enter the same transfer amount and destination in each provider.
2. Compare fee, exchange rate, delivery method, and arrival time.
3. Check whether the bonus applies to that exact route.
4. Read the minimum transfer amount and payout timing.
5. Choose the provider with the best overall result for your recipient.
6. Save the offer screen before completing the transfer.

## Checklist for comparing bonuses

- Same sender country, destination, amount, and payout method are used in each quote.
- Bonus type is clear: transfer credit, cashback, gift card, fee discount, or promo rate.
- Eligibility rules match the account and first transfer.
- Minimum transfer, expiry, and reward timing are clear.
- Verification and payment method requirements are manageable.
- Total received amount is compared after fees and exchange rate.

## Common mistakes

- Ignoring exchange-rate differences.
- Comparing a cash pickup offer with a bank deposit offer.
- Assuming a bonus is paid instantly.
- Choosing a provider only because a code exists.

## FAQ

### Should I choose the app with the biggest bonus?

Not automatically. A smaller bonus can still be better if the fee, exchange rate, and delivery method are stronger.

### Do all providers offer welcome bonuses?

No. Some providers may show local promotions, referral links, first-transfer offers, or no visible bonus for your account.

### What matters more than the bonus amount?

The total received amount, delivery method, transfer speed, verification requirements, and reward timing can matter more than the bonus amount.

### Can the best bonus vary by country?

Yes. Sender country, destination, payment method, and provider campaign rules can change which offer is best.
