# Goal 8 — Build Editorial Trust & Authority Signals

## Context

BonusFoundry has a strong technical foundation and well-structured content.

The next objective is to strengthen editorial trust signals for both users and AI systems.

This goal focuses on transparency, authority, structured identity and sourcing.

Do not invent qualifications or professional credentials.

---

## Objectives

Implement:

- Editorial Team identity
- Editorial Policy
- Research Methodology
- Organization Schema
- Person or Editorial Team Schema (following the chosen editorial model)
- Official Sources module
- Last Reviewed information
- Review metadata

Improve E-E-A-T and GEO signals without changing the site's editorial philosophy.

---

## Editorial Identity

Replace generic author information with a consistent editorial identity.

Preferred identity:

- BonusFoundry Editorial Team

Every article should clearly indicate:

- Reviewed by
- Editorial Team
- Last reviewed date

Do not create fictional qualifications.

The description should explain the editorial process rather than claim financial expertise.

Example responsibilities:

- research official provider documentation;
- review referral terms;
- verify help center articles;
- update pages when referral programs change.

---

## About Page

Improve or create an About page describing:

- what BonusFoundry is;
- editorial mission;
- independence;
- research methodology;
- update process;
- disclosure policy.

---

## Editorial Policy

Create a dedicated Editorial Policy page.

Include:

- source hierarchy;
- verification process;
- update frequency;
- correction policy;
- independence;
- disclosure;
- how referral information is verified.

---

## Research Methodology

Create a Research Methodology page explaining:

- official websites reviewed;
- referral terms;
- help centers;
- FAQ;
- legal documentation;
- support articles;
- update workflow.

Explain that BonusFoundry prioritizes official documentation over blogs or coupon sites.

---

## Official Sources

Create or improve a reusable "Official Sources" component.

Each provider page should expose:

- source title;
- source type;
- official link;
- what was verified;
- last checked date.

Do not use unofficial referral websites as sources.

---

## Last Reviewed

Display:

- Last reviewed
- Last updated (if available)

Use reusable components.

---

## Organization Schema

Implement Organization schema.

Include:

- name
- website
- logo
- description
- sameAs (official social profiles if available)

Connect the organization with the editorial pages.

---

## Editorial Schema

Implement an appropriate schema for the editorial identity.

If using an Editorial Team model, keep it consistent across the site.

Do not fabricate professional qualifications.

---

## Internal Linking

Link naturally between:

- About
- Editorial Policy
- Research Methodology
- Provider pages
- Guides
- FAQ

---

## Constraints

- Do not invent expertise.
- Do not invent certifications.
- Do not create fictional biographies.
- Preserve existing SEO.
- Reuse existing components where possible.

---

## Deliverables

1. About page created or improved.
2. Editorial Policy page.
3. Research Methodology page.
4. Organization schema implemented.
5. Editorial identity implemented.
6. Official Sources component improved.
7. Last Reviewed system implemented.
8. Internal linking updated.
9. Build passes.
10. Lint passes.
