# Goal 7 — Global Editorial & GEO Audit

## Context

The AI Content Design System has been implemented.

Provider Pages, Corridor Pages, Guides, FAQ and Country Hubs have been upgraded.

Now perform a complete editorial audit across the entire BonusFoundry website to improve consistency, authority and AI readability.

---

## Objectives

Review every page and ensure:

- consistent structure;
- consistent terminology;
- minimal repetition;
- stronger authority;
- better AI extraction;
- better user experience.

---

## Review All Content Types

Audit:

- Home page
- Provider pages
- Referral pages
- Corridor pages
- Country Hubs
- Guides
- FAQ
- About
- Any other editorial pages

---

## Reduce Repetition

Remove repeated wording across the site.

Avoid repeating the same explanations on every page.

Instead:

- summarize;
- link to dedicated pages;
- keep only corridor/provider-specific information.

---

## Improve Clarity

Answer the main question immediately.

Use direct wording whenever facts are verified.

Avoid unnecessary hedging.

Use conditional language only when information genuinely varies.

---

## Improve Consistency

Use the same terminology everywhere.

Examples:

- Referral Program
- Referral Code
- Referral Link
- Promo Code
- Welcome Bonus
- Signup Bonus
- Qualifying Transfer
- Mobile Money
- Cash Pickup
- Bank Deposit
- Corridor

Avoid unnecessary synonyms.

---

## Internal Linking

Review the entire internal linking strategy.

Ensure every page naturally links to relevant:

- Providers
- Corridors
- Guides
- FAQ
- Country Hubs

Avoid orphan pages.

Avoid duplicate navigation paths.

---

## AI Readability

Every section should be understandable on its own.

Avoid phrases such as:

- "As mentioned above"
- "As explained previously"

Instead, repeat the subject naturally.

Optimize every page for extraction by LLMs.

---

## Content Quality

Review all pages for:

- duplicate paragraphs;
- weak headings;
- unnecessary filler;
- inconsistent formatting;
- missing tables where useful;
- missing checklists;
- missing FAQs;
- outdated wording.

Improve only where it adds value.

---

## Glossary

Where useful, introduce short inline definitions for domain-specific terms on first use.

Examples:

- Corridor
- Referral
- Mobile Money
- Qualifying Transfer

Do not overuse definitions.

---

## Authority Signals

Ensure every page consistently exposes:

- Last Verified
- Official Sources
- Disclosure
- Related Resources

Reuse the components created in Goal 1.

---

## Performance

Do not introduce unnecessary JavaScript.

Keep pages static-first.

Preserve Lighthouse performance.

---

## Constraints

- Do not change URLs.
- Do not break SEO.
- Do not remove structured data.
- Do not duplicate content.
- Do not invent facts.
- Reuse existing components.

---

## Deliverables

1. Pages reviewed.
2. Editorial inconsistencies fixed.
3. Repetitive content reduced.
4. Internal linking improved.
5. Terminology standardized.
6. AI readability improved.
7. Build passes.
8. Lint passes.
