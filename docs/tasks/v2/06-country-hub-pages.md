# Goal 6 — Upgrade All Country Hub Pages

## Context

Upgrade all Country Hub pages using the AI Content Design System.

Reuse existing components.

Do not redesign the site.

---

## Objectives

Make every Country Hub:

- more useful;
- easier to scan;
- optimized for AI extraction;
- the main entry point for users sending money from that country.

Each country should have its own identity.

---

## Apply Components

Use where relevant:

- Quick Answer
- Key Takeaways
- Country Summary
- Featured Providers
- Popular Corridors
- Provider Quick Cards
- Best For
- Common Mistakes
- Mini FAQ
- Related Resources
- Official Sources
- Last Verified

---

## Improve Structure

Suggested order:

1. Quick Answer
2. Key Takeaways
3. Country Summary
4. Featured Providers
5. Popular Corridors
6. Best For
7. Common Mistakes
8. FAQ
9. Related Resources

---

## Featured Providers

For each provider, display a compact card including:

- Reward
- Eligibility
- Minimum Transfer
- Best For
- Last Verified

Link to the full provider page.

---

## Popular Corridors

Highlight the most relevant sending corridors.

Do not repeat the same generic text.

Each corridor should briefly explain why it matters for users in that country.

---

## Editorial Rules

Keep content country-specific.

Mention only providers, payment methods and corridors relevant to that country.

Avoid repeating provider descriptions already covered elsewhere.

Link to provider and corridor pages for details.

---

## Internal Linking

Strengthen links between:

- Country Hubs
- Providers
- Corridors
- Guides
- FAQ

Avoid orphan pages.

---

## Constraints

- Keep existing URLs.
- Preserve SEO metadata.
- Preserve structured data.
- Reuse components from Goal 1.
- Do not duplicate provider content.

---

## Deliverables

1. Country Hub pages updated.
2. Components reused.
3. Internal linking improved.
4. Repetitive content reduced.
5. Build passes.
6. Lint passes.
